You are hosting a party and you want to personally greet each of your friends in a unique way when they arrive. You decided to use their name and their favorite drink in your greeting. Write a program that takes two inputs, friend's name and their favorite drink. Your program should print a message whichsays, "Hello [Friend's name], I hope you are having a wonderful day! Would you like a glass of [Favorite drink]?". Only the first letter of the friend's name and the drink should be capitalized.


Sample Input:
John
Water

Sample Output:
Hello John, I hope you are having a wonderful day! Would you like a glass of Water?


Sample Input:
marie
coffee

Sample Output:
Hello Marie, I hope you are having a wonderful day! Would you like a glass of Coffee?



Memory limit: 256 MB
Time limit: 15 seconds
